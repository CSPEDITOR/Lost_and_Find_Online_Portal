PORTAL

A Lost and Found Online Portal for a college serves as a digital platform to facilitate the reporting and retrieval of lost or found items within the college campus community. The portal provides an efficient and organized way for students, staff, and faculty to report items they have lost or found, enhancing the chances of reuniting owners with their belongings. The portal typically includes features such as:

Reporting Lost and Found Items:

Users can submit detailed information about lost or found items through an online form. This includes item descriptions, locations, and contact information.

Search Functionality:

The portal offers search capabilities to help users find items that have been reported as lost or found. Users can filter search results based on various criteria.

User Profile

Users may have the option to create profiles within the portal, which can store their contact details and keep track of items they have reported or found.
